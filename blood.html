WHO CAN GIVE BLOOD

*CHECK YOUR ELIGIBILITY*
You can give blood if:

  • You are fit and healthy :)

  • You weigh between 7 stone 12 lbs and 25 stone, or 50kg and 160kg :)

  • You are aged between 17 and 66 (or 70 if you have given blood before) :)

  • You are over 70 and have given blood in the last two years :)

The common reasons donors should check if they can give blood are:
  • If you are receiving medical or hospital treatment

  • If you are taking medication

  • During and after pregnancy

  • If you feel ill

  • if you have cancer

  • After receiving blood, blood products or organs.
